LAZARUS Learning

What fun is building your own super computer if you don't get to tell anybody about it? The LAZARUS Team is working hard to spread the word about our exciting work.

Groups of all ages and sizes have enjoyed experiencing LAZARUS for themselves. Below is a list of groups and programs who have visited LAZARUS. If you are interested in setting up a tour or a visit to LAZARUS to talk with our faculty, staff or students, please contact us to discuss details.

  • Anton High School
  • Anton Middle School
  • Texas Tech Summer College Experience
  • Emmy Noether Day Students and Faculty
  • PRISM Summer Research Weeks
  • Careers in Science
  • Texas Tech Summer Math Academy
